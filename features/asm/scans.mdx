## Overview

Scans & Scheduling lets you automate asset discovery with flexible scan configurations.  
Initiate scans manually or set recurring schedules to keep your attack surface inventory updated.  
Visualize discovery trends over time to understand exposure growth and asset lifecycle.

Keep your attack surface mapping consistent and always fresh.  
Scheduled scans ensure every newly deployed asset is identified and assessed promptly.
