## Overview

DNS Record Mapping gives you a unified view of all DNS entries — including A, MX, TXT, and SOA records — across your environment.  
Snapsec continuously tracks and analyzes DNS changes to detect misconfigurations, exposed services, or hijack risks before they impact your infrastructure.
