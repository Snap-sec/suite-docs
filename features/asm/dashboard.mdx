## Overview

The Unified Attack Surface Dashboard offers a complete view of your organization’s internet-facing footprint.  
It aggregates all discovered assets — subdomains, IPs, ports, DNS records, certificates, and exposures — into a single, intuitive dashboard that visualizes key trends and metrics in real time.

Replace fragmented asset tracking with a single source of truth for your attack surface.  
The dashboard empowers teams to detect exposure trends, prioritize high-risk assets, and maintain real-time visibility into security posture changes.
