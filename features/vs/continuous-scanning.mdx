## Overview

Continuous Scanning enables teams to automate recurring vulnerability assessments across their environments.  
By scheduling weekly, monthly, or fully customized scan intervals, you ensure uninterrupted visibility into new risks and maintain a consistent security baseline across all assets.

## Value

Ensure vulnerabilities are identified as soon as they appear.  
Continuous scanning eliminates long gaps between assessments, strengthens your security posture, and enables proactive remediation across your infrastructure.
