## Overview

The Centralized Threat Dashboard provides a single, real-time view of all threats across your applications and services.  
From active and pending threats to acknowledged, resolved, and fix-verified issues — the dashboard brings full visibility into severity levels, CWE groupings, and ongoing attack activity.

## Value

The Centralized Threat Dashboard empowers engineering and security teams with instant visibility into threats across all projects.  
By combining real-time activity, severity analytics, and lifecycle progression, it accelerates triage, improves decision-making, and strengthens overall threat management posture.
