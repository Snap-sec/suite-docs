## Overview

Access & Policy Controls give teams fine-grained authority over who can view, edit, or administer threat-modeling projects.\
With project-level roles and customizable threat-generation policies, organizations can enforce secure collaboration, maintain consistent modeling standards, and ensure the quality of all generated threat data.

## Value

Access & Policy Controls ensure secure, structured, and consistent threat modeling across your organization.\
By combining role-based access with customizable threat-generation policies, teams maintain governance, high-quality outputs, and clear accountability across all projects.
