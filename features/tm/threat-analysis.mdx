## Overview

Threat Analysis provides deep, structured insight into every identified threat.  
Each threat contains full context — including explanations, severity, confidence, evidence, request metadata, mitigations, and activity history — enabling teams to investigate effectively and resolve issues with precision.

Threat Analysis equips teams with everything they need to understand, validate, and remediate threats effectively.  
With structured explanations, evidence, request context, and full audit history, teams can make accurate decisions and resolve issues faster.
