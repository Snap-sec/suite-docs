## Overview

Threat Metrics & Reporting provides leadership and engineering teams with rich visual insights into threat status, severity distribution, progress, and overall security posture.  
With project-level and global dashboards, teams can easily compare trends, monitor remediation velocity, and identify bottlenecks across environments and services.

## Value

Threat Metrics & Reporting provides complete visibility into threat posture and remediation performance.  
With severity trends, lifecycle metrics, and comparative dashboards, leaders and engineers gain the clarity needed to prioritize efforts, track improvements, and strengthen organizational security maturity.
