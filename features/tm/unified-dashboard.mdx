## Overview

The Unified Threat Dashboard provides a real-time, organization-wide overview of all threats across applications and microservices.\
By combining severity distribution, CWE mappings, workload progress, and lifecycle insights, the dashboard helps teams quickly identify risk hotspots, track ongoing activity, and make informed remediation decisions.

## Value

The Unified Threat Dashboard centralizes all threat intelligence into a single, actionable interface.\
It equips engineering and security teams with immediate visibility into organization-wide risks, enabling faster decision-making, better prioritization, and more effective remediation.
