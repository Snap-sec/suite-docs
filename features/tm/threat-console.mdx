## Overview

Structured Threat Review gives teams a centralized and highly organized interface for evaluating all threats within a project or across the organization.\
With advanced filtering, sorting, and metadata-rich rows, teams can quickly triage threats, prioritize workload, and maintain complete visibility throughout the remediation process.

## Value

Structured Threat Review centralizes all threat evaluation into a fast, intuitive, and powerful triage console.\
Teams gain clarity, reduce noise, and efficiently prioritize remediation efforts across complex systems and multiple projects.
