## Overview

Ownership & Collaboration Tracking ensures every threat has clear accountability and real-time communication across engineering and security teams.  
From ownership assignment and activity logs to discussions and updates, this feature streamlines coordination and accelerates remediation workflows.

## Value

Improve remediation speed and eliminate bottlenecks through clear ownership and seamless collaboration.  
With real-time updates, activity logs, and structured communication, teams work more efficiently and maintain complete accountability throughout the threat lifecycle.
