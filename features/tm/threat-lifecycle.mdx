## Overview

The Structured Threat Lifecycle provides a clear, disciplined workflow for managing threats from discovery to verification.  
Each threat progresses through well-defined states — ensuring visibility, accountability, and proper remediation tracking across engineering and security teams.

## Key Capabilities

## Value

Bring structure, clarity, and accountability to the threat management process.  
A well-defined lifecycle helps teams prioritize efficiently, resolve threats faster, and maintain long-term consistency across projects and microservices.
