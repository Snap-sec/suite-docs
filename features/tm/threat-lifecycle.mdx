## Overview

Threat Lifecycle & Ownership Management ensures complete traceability and accountability throughout the threat’s journey.\
Each threat progresses through structured states with assigned owners, real-time updates, and detailed audit logs — enabling teams to collaborate efficiently and maintain full visibility over remediation progress.

## Value

Threat Lifecycle & Ownership Management provides reliability, structure, and transparency across the entire remediation process.\
Teams work more effectively with clear ownership, real-time progress tracking, and detailed audit trails — ensuring threats move from discovery to verification without delays.
