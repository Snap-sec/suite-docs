## Overview

Multi-Project Threat Consolidation provides a unified, organization-wide view of all threats across applications, services, and microservices.  
This centralized perspective enables teams to analyze exposure, track progress, and quickly identify high-risk areas without switching between projects or dashboards.

## Value

Multi-Project Threat Consolidation gives leadership and engineering teams a complete, cross-project view of their threat landscape.  
With unified filtering, state tracking, and severity insights, organizations can prioritize at scale, eliminate blind spots, and strengthen their overall security posture.
