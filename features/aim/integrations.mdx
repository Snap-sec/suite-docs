## Overview

Automated Discovery & Integrations eliminate the manual effort of asset collection by continuously importing data from connected sources.  
Whether it’s domains from Cloudflare, repositories from GitHub, or APIs from Postman, AIM adapts to your ecosystem using native and open adapters.  
This ensures that your inventory stays up-to-date and complete without requiring repetitive manual input.
