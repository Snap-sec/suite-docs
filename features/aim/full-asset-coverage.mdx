## Overview

Comprehensive Asset Intelligence provides a complete understanding of your digital ecosystem.  
AIM unifies every asset — from applications and cloud infrastructure to code repositories and employee workstations — under one continuously updated inventory.  
It helps security, DevOps, and compliance teams maintain an accurate, actionable map of all assets with context, ownership, and risk.

By consolidating every asset type into one intelligent platform, AIM breaks down silos between engineering, security, and compliance.  
It provides continuous discovery, classification, and risk visibility — ensuring nothing remains hidden across your attack surface.
