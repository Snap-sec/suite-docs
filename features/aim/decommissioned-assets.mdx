## Overview

Decommissioned Asset Lifecycle ensures no retired or inactive asset gets lost.  
It keeps historical data, tracks decommissioning reasons, and provides full traceability for reactivation, auditing, or compliance.

Ensures complete asset traceability, reduces data loss, and enforces accountability even after assets are phased out.
