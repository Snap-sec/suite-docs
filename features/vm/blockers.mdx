## Overview

Blocker Management ensures that remediation challenges are tracked, discussed, and resolved transparently.  
When a vulnerability cannot be fixed immediately, teams can record and manage blockers to maintain accountability and visibility.

## Key Capabilities

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Track Blockers" icon="ban">
    Log blockers such as business dependencies, risk acceptance, or pending validation directly within each vulnerability.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collaborative Resolution" icon="users">
    Enable security and development teams to comment, review, and approve blocker resolutions collectively.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Severity Revalidation" icon="gauge-high">
    Reassess the impact or severity of blocked findings to ensure prioritization stays accurate over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accountability & Audit" icon="file-shield">
    Maintain a full history of blocker discussions and approvals for compliance and audit readiness.
  </Card>
</Columns>
