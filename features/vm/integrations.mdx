## Overview

The Integration Ecosystem connects Snapsec VM with your existing tools, ensuring data flows seamlessly across your security and development stack.  
Automate vulnerability imports, synchronize tickets, and maintain a single source of truth across your ecosystem.

## Key Capabilities
