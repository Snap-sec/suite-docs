## How AIM Uses Censys Data

AIM automatically:

- Detects **external-facing assets**  
- Flags **exposures** such as open ports or public services  
- Updates **asset ownership**, **environment**, and **risk classification**  
- Correlates IPs/domains with existing vulnerabilities  
- Expands discovery for related assets (recursive enrichment)

This ensures your external attack surface remains fully mapped and monitored.
