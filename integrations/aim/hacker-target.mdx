## Overview

The **HackerTarget integration** allows AIM to gather reconnaissance data for your domains using HackerTarget’s public scanning endpoints.  
This helps enrich asset discovery by identifying additional exposed services, open ports, and DNS information discovered externally.

---

## Prerequisites

Before enabling the integration:

- Ensure you have a valid **domain** added to AIM.
- Confirm your environment allows outbound HTTPS requests to HackerTarget.
- No API key is required for standard enumeration.

---

## How to Enable HackerTarget in AIM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open AIM Integrations" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Go to **AIM → Integrations** in the Snapsec sidebar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select HackerTarget" icon="satellite-dish">
    Find the **HackerTarget** integration tile and click **Install**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Installation" icon="circle-check">
    Click **Install Now** to activate the integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once enabled, AIM begins fetching external enumeration data for supported domains.

---

## What Data HackerTarget Provides

HackerTarget can enrich AIM with:

- DNS Records  
- Subdomain enumeration  
- Open port data  
- Basic service fingerprinting  

This information helps expand your visibility into externally exposed infrastructure.

---

## Managing the Integration

After installation:

- The integration will appear under **Active Adapters**.
- You can **refresh**, **pause**, or **remove** the adapter at any time.
- Logs of each fetch run are available under **Refresh Logs**.
