## Snapsec AIM integration with Snapsec ASM

The **Snapsec AIM ‚Üí ASM integration** allows ASM to automatically ingest internal assets, enrich discovered external assets, and correlate ownership, tags, and classifications directly from AIM.

With this integration, your external attack surface gains internal context ‚Äî enabling stronger prioritization, faster triage, and unified asset visibility across the organization.

---

## 1. What this integration does

Once enabled, ASM will automatically:

- Import **internal assets** (domains, IPs, APIs, applications, certificates, technologies, etc.) from AIM.  
- Enrich ASM-discovered assets with:
  - Ownership  
  - Environment tags  
  - Risk metadata  
  - Asset groups  
  - Exposure classification  
- Prevent duplicates by correlating assets across both systems.
- Improve prioritization during exposure analysis by providing internal inventory context.

This helps security teams quickly understand **which internet-facing assets belong to whom**, and whether they tie back to known infrastructure.

---

## 2. Prerequisites

To enable the integration, you need:

- A Snapsec account with access to both **AIM** and **ASM** modules.
- Permissions to install/configure integrations.
- An active AIM workspace containing asset data.

No API keys are required ‚Äî Snapsec handles authentication internally.

---

## 3. Enable the AIM integration in ASM

1. Go to **ASM ‚Üí Integrations**.  
2. Locate **Snapsec AIM** in the integrations list.  
3. Click **Install** or **Configure**.  
4. Review any sync options (depending on workspace settings):
   - Sync domains  
   - Sync IP addresses  
   - Sync certificates  
   - Sync repositories  
   - Sync applications & APIs  
   - Sync tags & ownership  
5. Click **Save**.

Your ASM workspace will immediately begin synchronizing with AIM.

## 6. Troubleshooting

### AIM assets not showing in ASM?
- Ensure sync options (Domains / IPs / Certs / APIs etc.) are enabled.
- Check if AIM assets are marked active.
- Look for duplicate suppression when ASM already discovered an asset externally.

### Metadata not updating?
- Confirm the asset exists in AIM with correct tags/owner.
- Run **manual refresh**.

### Integration not connecting?
- Ensure your workspace role has integration permissions.
