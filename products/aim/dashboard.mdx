## Overview

The **Asset Intelligence Management (AIM) Dashboard** acts as your command center for monitoring enterprise assets in real time.  
From subdomains and APIs to repositories and certificates — this dashboard consolidates insights from all connected adapters and data sources.

Each section provides actionable visibility: where assets come from, who owns them, and what technologies they use.

## Dashboard Insights

### Asset Distribution
A tabular summary of asset types and counts, helping teams quickly understand where assets are concentrated (e.g., 192 Subdomains, 173 Applications).

### Severity Distribution
Real-time visualization of asset risk levels — instantly shows how many assets are associated with critical or high issues.

### Ownership Overview
Track asset ownership percentage. Quickly spot unassigned assets that need owners to ensure complete accountability.

### Adapter Overview
List of all configured adapters with their usage count — easily verify integrations with external sources like DNS records, certificates, and vulnerability data.

### Technology Insights
See which technologies appear most frequently across your assets, helping with stack standardization and potential security policy alignment.
