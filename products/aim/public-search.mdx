| Type | Description | Recommended For |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| **Global Asset Search** | Read-only view to explore assets and vulnerabilities. | Auditors, Viewers, Compliance Teams |
| **Edit Asset Link** | Editable view to update metadata fields like Owner, Exposure, or Environment. | Internal Teams, Asset Managers |

## Best Practices

- Limit public link sharing to trusted users or teams.  
- Use the **read-only link** for transparency and reporting.  
- Periodically regenerate or disable old links to maintain access control.  
- Track edits through audit logs for accountability.
