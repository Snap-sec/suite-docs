## Overview

The **Vendor Directory** provides a complete overview of all external vendors connected to your organization.  
AIM automatically aggregates vendors from integrations, employee accounts, and platform links — giving teams the visibility needed to monitor risk, ownership, and dependency footprint.

## Key Metrics

The Vendor Directory highlights essential insights:

- **Total Vendors** – Count of all third-party vendors detected.
- **Risk Level** – Shows which vendors have associated risks.
- **Employee Associations** – Number of internal accounts linked to each vendor.
- **Descriptions & Metadata** – Quick contextual details such as services, domains, and categories.

---

## Vendor Breakdown

Each vendor card includes:

### **1. Vendor Name & Logo**  
Automatically fetched where available for visual clarity.

### **2. Risk Status**  
Indicates whether the vendor presents any associated risk signals.

### **3. Associated Employees**  
Shows how many internal users interact with or belong to the vendor (e.g., SSO integrations, linked emails).  
Clicking the employee count opens a list of associated profiles.

### **4. Details & Metadata**  
Each vendor includes a *See more details* link for expanded insights.
