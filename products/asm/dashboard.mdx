## Overview

The **ASM Dashboard** provides a real-time, consolidated view of your organization’s external attack surface.

It highlights **what assets exist**, **where risks are emerging**, and **how your exposure is evolving over time** — enabling teams to quickly understand and act on security posture.

Unlike static inventories, this dashboard continuously reflects data from discovery and scanning pipelines, ensuring decisions are based on the **latest observed surface**.

## Key Metrics

The top section provides a quick snapshot of your current attack surface:

| Metric | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Total Assets** | Total number of discovered assets across your environment |
| **Risky Assets** | Assets currently associated with identified risks or exposures |
| **Critical Risks** | High-impact issues requiring immediate attention |
| **Next Scan** | Scheduled time for the next discovery or scan cycle |

These metrics help teams instantly assess **scale, risk concentration, and upcoming activity**.

---

## Risk & Asset Trend

The **Asset vs Risk Trend** graph visualizes how your attack surface evolves over time.

- Tracks **growth of assets** vs **increase in risks**
- Helps identify whether risk is scaling with discovery
- Useful for measuring **security posture improvement or degradation**

<Callout icon="chart-line" color="blue">
A rising risk curve alongside asset growth may indicate unmanaged exposure or delayed remediation.
</Callout>

## Asset Distribution

The **Asset Distribution panel** breaks down your attack surface by asset type and highlights associated risks.

### Includes:

- Ports  
- Web Servers  
- DNS Records  
- Technologies  
- IP Addresses  
- Subdomains  
- Certificates  

Each row shows:

- Total count per asset type  
- Visual distribution bar  
- Highlighted risk indicators (where applicable)  

This allows teams to quickly identify **which asset categories contribute most to exposure**.

---

## Top Critical Risks

The **Top Critical Risks table** provides a focused view of high-priority issues.

| Field | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Asset Type** | The affected asset (e.g., subdomain or host) |
| **Reason** | Context or description of the identified issue |
| **State** | Current status (e.g., In Review, False Positive) |
| **Severity** | Risk level associated with the issue |

This section helps teams:

- Prioritize remediation efforts  
- Track validation status  
- Quickly review critical findings without navigating deeper  

<Callout icon="triangle-exclamation" color="red">
Critical risks should be reviewed and validated promptly to prevent exploitable exposure.
</Callout>
