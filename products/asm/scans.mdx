## How Scans Work

Each scan performs a **multi-layered discovery process**, combining:
1. **Passive Discovery** â€” certificate transparency logs, WHOIS data, ASN mappings, public datasets.  
2. **Active Enumeration** â€” DNS brute-forcing, subdomain resolvers, and HTTP fingerprinting.  
3. **Asset Enrichment** â€” correlating discovered items with IPs, ports, and technologies.  
4. **Exposure Detection** â€” scanning for misconfigurations, open ports, and public access.

All discovered assets are automatically de-duplicated and enriched before being reflected in your **Asset Catalog**.
