## Overview

**Tickets** provide a centralized workflow to **track, assign, and remediate security exposures** identified by Risk Signals.

Each exposure can be converted into an actionable ticket, enabling teams to move from detection to resolution efficiently.

## Workflow Example

1. Exposure is detected via Risk Signals  
2. A ticket is created automatically or manually  
3. Security team reviews details and assigns ownership  
4. Actions are taken (fix, mitigate, or escalate)  
5. Ticket is synced with VM or external systems  
6. Status is updated and validated
