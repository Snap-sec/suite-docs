## Overview

Settings allow you to integrate your Asset Inventory with the Vulnerability Scanner and control how vulnerabilities are synchronized, categorized, and stored.

This ensures alignment between scanning results and your broader security assessments.

- Post-Remediation Validation  
- Business Logic Testing

---

## Severity-Based Sync

<Frame caption="Select which severity levels should be automatically synchronized">
  <img src="/images/vs/settings-3.png" />
</Frame>

Control which vulnerabilities are pushed into assessments:

- **Critical** — Always prioritize  
- **High** — Important risks  
- **Medium** — Moderate impact  
- **Low** — Minor issues  
- **Info** — Informational findings  

This allows teams to focus only on actionable risks.
