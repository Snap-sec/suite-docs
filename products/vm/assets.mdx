## Overview

The **Asset Catalog** provides a centralized, real-time view of all assets discovered across your environment — including subdomains, applications, IPs, and infrastructure components.

It combines **asset intelligence + vulnerability context** into a single interface, allowing teams to quickly understand:

- What assets exist  
- Which assets are vulnerable  
- How risk is distributed  
- What needs immediate attention  

Each asset is enriched with ownership, vulnerability counts, and remediation progress — enabling faster prioritization and accountability.

<Callout icon="shield-check" color="green">
Every asset is continuously mapped to vulnerabilities — giving you a live view of your attack surface risk.
</Callout>

## Asset Intelligence Dashboard

<Frame caption="High-level asset insights including total assets, vulnerable assets, and distribution charts">
  <img src="/images/vm/asset-catlog2.png" alt="Asset insights dashboard showing distribution and vulnerability metrics" />

The top section provides a **quick snapshot of your entire asset landscape**.

### Key Insights

- **Total Assets**
  - Total number of discovered assets across all sources  

- **Vulnerable Assets**
  - Assets currently affected by one or more vulnerabilities  

- **Safe Assets**
  - Assets with no active vulnerabilities  

---

### Visual Breakdowns

- **Asset Type Distribution**
  - Understand the composition of your environment (subdomains, IPs, apps, storage, etc.)  

- **Vulnerable vs Safe Assets**
  - Instantly assess overall exposure  

- **Severity Distribution**
  - Identify where critical, high, and low-risk assets exist  

<Callout icon="chart-pie" color="blue">
These visual insights help security teams prioritize high-risk asset categories first.
</Callout>

## Asset Inventory Table

The asset table provides a **detailed, actionable view** of every asset.

### Key Columns

- **Asset Name**
  - Domain, subdomain, or resource identifier  

- **Owner**
  - Assigned team or user responsible for the asset  

- **Total Vulnerabilities**
  - Total number of findings linked to the asset  

- **Open Vulnerabilities**
  - Breakdown by severity (e.g., High, Low, Info)  

- **Remediation Progress**
  - Percentage of resolved vulnerabilities  

<Callout icon="eye" color="blue">
Click any asset to drill down into its full vulnerability list and detailed findings.
</Callout>

## Vulnerability Correlation

Each asset is automatically linked to vulnerabilities identified across:

- Assessments  
- Scanners and integrations  
- Manual testing  

This ensures that every finding is tied to its originating asset, giving complete traceability and context.

<Callout icon="chart-diagram" color="pink">
Assets and vulnerabilities stay continuously synchronized — no manual linking required.
</Callout>
