# Vulnerability Management (VM)

The **Vulnerability Management (VM)** module is a comprehensive solution for tracking, prioritizing, and remediating vulnerabilities across your organization's assets.

## Key Features

- **Centralized Vulnerability Tracking**: Monitor vulnerabilities across all your assets in one unified dashboard
- **Risk-Based Prioritization**: Automatically prioritize vulnerabilities based on CVSS scores, asset criticality, and business impact
- **SLA Management**: Set and track Service Level Agreements for vulnerability remediation
- **Assessment Management**: Organize vulnerability assessments and track their progress
- **Asset Integration**: Seamlessly connect with discovered assets from ASM and AIM modules
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Generate comprehensive reports for stakeholders and compliance

## Core Concepts

### Vulnerabilities
Individual security issues found in your assets, each with:
- CVSS scores and severity ratings
- Detailed descriptions and remediation guidance
- Status tracking (Open, In Progress, Resolved, False Positive)
- Assignment to responsible teams

### Assessments
Structured evaluations of your assets for vulnerabilities:
- Scheduled or on-demand assessments
- Assessment groups for organizing related evaluations
- Progress tracking and completion status

### Assets
The systems, applications, and infrastructure you're protecting:
- Asset discovery and classification
- Business context and ownership
- Risk scoring and criticality levels

## Integration Points

VM integrates seamlessly with other Snapsec Suite modules:

- **ASM**: Import discovered assets for vulnerability tracking
- **AIM**: Enrich assets with business context and ownership
- **VS**: Import scan results and vulnerability data
- **TM**: Correlate vulnerabilities with threat intelligence
