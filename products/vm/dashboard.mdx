## Overview

The **Dashboard** acts as the central command center for your vulnerability management program.  
It provides a real-time snapshot of your security posture, helping teams quickly understand what needs attention, what’s in progress, and what’s already resolved.

---

## What You Can See

- **Total Vulnerabilities**
  - Instantly view the total number of detected vulnerabilities across your environment.

- **Status Distribution**
  - Track vulnerabilities across stages like *In Review, Triaged, Retest, Resolved, and Rejected*.

- **Detection vs Resolution Timeline**
  - Visualize how vulnerabilities are being discovered and fixed over time.

- **Exportable Reports**
  - Generate and export reports directly from the dashboard for audits and stakeholder updates.

---

## Insights & Analytics

<Frame caption="Detailed analytics and performance insights">
  <img
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/vm/d2.png"
  />
</Frame>

The dashboard provides deeper insights to help teams track performance and prioritize remediation effectively.

### Key Insights

- **Severity Breakdown**
  - Understand the distribution of vulnerabilities across *Critical, High, Medium, Low,* and *Informational*.

- **Open vs Closed by Severity**
  - Compare how many vulnerabilities are still open versus resolved across each severity level.

- **SLA Compliance**
  - Monitor whether vulnerabilities are being resolved within defined SLA timelines.

- **Top Performing Members**
  - Identify contributors driving the most vulnerability resolutions.

- **Business Unit Visibility**
  - Analyze how vulnerabilities are distributed across different business units.

- **Department-wise Remediation**
  - Track how each department is performing in resolving assigned vulnerabilities.

---

## Why It Matters

The dashboard brings clarity to complex vulnerability data by turning it into actionable insights.  
It enables teams to prioritize effectively, track progress continuously, and ensure accountability across the organization — all from a single view.
