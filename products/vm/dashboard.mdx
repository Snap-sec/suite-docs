## Overview

The **Unified Vulnerability Dashboard** serves as your command center for everything related to vulnerability management.  
It consolidates vulnerabilities, remediation progress, and performance analytics into one intuitive interface — giving both security and engineering teams instant visibility into the current risk posture.

From tracking the number of vulnerabilities under review or resolved, to monitoring remediation trends and departmental performance, the dashboard helps you make data-driven security decisions with confidence.  
Clear visual metrics and charts help you identify bottlenecks, measure SLA compliance, and understand how security improvements are progressing across your organization.
