## On-Demand Testing

Run **instant vulnerability scans** whenever needed — directly from the dashboard, via CI/CD pipelines, or through API triggers.

<Check>
  Launch ad-hoc scans for assets during development or after new deployments
</Check>

<Check>
  Integrate with CI/CD to automatically scan every commit, build, or release
</Check>

<Check>
  Validate fixes immediately by rescanning specific assets
</Check>

<Check>
  Get near real-time visibility into security drift or newly introduced risks
</Check>

---

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Scan Target">
    Choose an individual asset, asset group, or API collection.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Scan">
    Trigger the scan manually or let CI/CD fire it automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Processing">
    The scanner analyzes technologies, services, misconfigurations, and vulnerabilities.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Instant Results">
    Findings appear immediately with severity, proof, and remediation insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
